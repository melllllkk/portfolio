My initial exposure to programming occurred during my sophomore year of high school when I enrolled in AP CSA. I struggled with effectively employing the concepts to develop programs capable of solving problem sets. This marked the first instance in which I felt genuinely defeated during an examination. Nevertheless, this setback served as motivation for me to strive for a deeper understanding in programming.
